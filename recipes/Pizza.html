Pizza

Description

Pizza is a classic Italian dish consisting of a yeasted flatbread typically topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It's a versatile and beloved dish enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup warm water (110째F/43째C)
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine yeast, sugar, and warm water. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Mix until a dough forms, then knead on a floured surface for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours or until doubled in size.
  5. Punch down the dough, then roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  6. Preheat the oven to 475째F (245째C).
  7. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  8. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  9. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce and add your favorite toppings.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
